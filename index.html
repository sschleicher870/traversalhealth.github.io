Correctional Oncology, Reimagined

Cancer care, delivered inside the walls.

Traversal Health brings medical oncology care—including expertise, coordination, 24/7 support, and treatment—into correctional facilities directly to meet patients, medical teams, and necessary custodial support where they are. Our model replaces challenging off-site care to deliver better outcomes for patients, fewer off-site transports required for the system, and overall higher value and lower total cost of care.
